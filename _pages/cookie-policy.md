This Cookie Policy explains how The Pulse ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website at thepulse.example.com.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the Site to function properly. They cannot be disabled. They include cookies that:

- Remember your preferences during a session
- Enable secure areas of the Site to function

### Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our Site. This helps us improve our content and user experience. We use:

- **Google Analytics** — Tracks page views, session duration, and user behavior. Data is anonymized where possible.

### Advertising Cookies

If we display advertisements, advertising networks may place cookies to:

- Deliver relevant advertisements
- Limit how often you see an ad
- Measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns

These may include cookies from **Google AdSense** and other advertising partners.

### Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the Site to remember choices you make (such as your preferred language) and provide enhanced features.

## Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. We do not control these cookies. Please refer to the respective third-party privacy policies for more information:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google AdSense](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

## Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

### Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

- View cookies stored on your device
- Delete cookies
- Block cookies from specific sites
- Block all third-party cookies

Refer to your browser's help documentation for instructions.

### Opt-Out Tools

- **Google Analytics:** [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- **Google Ads:** [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com)

### Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Site. Strictly necessary cookies cannot be disabled without impacting Site performance.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
